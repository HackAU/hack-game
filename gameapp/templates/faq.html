What is The Game?

The Game is a side event during HackUPC, consisting of few challenges that you have to solve in the given time. These challenges can involve maths, code and multimedia. There are no limits in the tools you can use. Any anouncements will be given through #thegame channel on HackUPC Slack.

Are there any prizes?

Top 1 will get a Pebble Smartwatch. Top 10 people in the ranking will be awarded an exclusive The Game T-shirt!

My solution is wrong

Keep trying, you may be close to the answer! Note that all solutions are lowercase, and only contain letters from english alphabet, and numbers.

The maximum length is 130 characters as well.

But I'm sure my solution is correct!

It can happen that we made a mistake. If you strongly believe our solution is wrong, please let us know on #thegame channel on slack. Any correction we make during the event will be announced on slack.

My solution is so slow!

Try to come up with a better approach :). All expected solutions run in under 10 seconds on a light laptop. Most of the solutions run in less than a second.
